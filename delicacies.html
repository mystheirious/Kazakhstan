Most Renowned Kazakh Dishes

Rich Culinary Heritage

Beshbarmak

Kazakhstan’s national dish, consisting of boiled meat over flat noodles in an onion broth. Traditionally eaten by hand.

Kazy

A traditional horse meat sausage, often served during celebrations, with a rich, smoky flavor.

Kuurdak

A hearty dish made from lamb or beef offal, sautéed with onions and spices, typically eaten fresh.

Baursak

Deep-fried dough balls, similar to donuts, often served during festive occasions.

Shashlik

Marinated skewered meat, grilled over open flames, popular across Central Asia.

Lagman

A noodle dish with stir-fried meat and vegetables in a flavorful sauce, influenced by Uyghur cuisine.

Manti

Steamed dumplings filled with minced lamb or beef, often served with sour cream.

Qurt

Dried salted cheese balls, a popular portable snack among Kazakh nomads.
